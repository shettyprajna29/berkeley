CSR

Berkeley Capital Group cares.

We strive not only to deliver financial performance, but also to make a positive contribution to society. In particular, we are committed to the success of all stakeholders, including the wider community and our employees.

Causes We Care About

1. Diversity

We are committed to promoting a diverse and inclusive culture at Berkeley Capital Group. In particular, we support the progression of women into senior roles in the company by focusing on the executive and the mid-tier level pipeline, and are committed to work collaboratively with others to tackle barriers to diversity.

2. Sustainability

We are committed to minimising the environmental impact of our business and maximising the efficiencies of our assets. As such, we conduct development work responsibly and give proper consideration to environmental, sustainability and social matters.”
